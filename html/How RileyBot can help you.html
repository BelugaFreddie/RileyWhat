How RileyBot can help you

Spelling

It can help you by telling you how to spell something and sounding it out for you.

Vocabulary

Rileybot can make you a wordbank based on a story you tell it.
If you take a picture with writing, Rileybot can identify the spelling errors and grammar errors and help you improve them.

Feedback

Rileybot can give you feedback on a piece of writing or other things like a piece of art.
You can also give feedback to RileyBot which will improve its functionality.

Research

Rileybot can help you with research by listing facts about the things you tell it so you can use them in a fact file or an information report.

Maths-Draw Function

You can draw a diagram to help Rileybot to understand you in the way you want.
You can also draw a equation with an explanation on how you want it to be solved.

Reading out loud

If you can't read something you can take a picture and Rileybot can read it to you.
You can hold on a paragraph Rileybot has displayed and click read aloud and it will read it aloud to you. This might help with practicing spellings.

Roleplay

Rileybot can give you ideas on a play script or it can help you make one.
It can also give you some prop ideas based on your story.
